ABOUT US





Unistack started off in February 2015 as a partner product of Stupidsid to help students who wish to pursue masters abroad. Since then, Unistack has been the quintessential ‘study abroad’ partner for lakhs of students. Our mobile application and web interface have become the constant companion of students applying abroad. With our services now branching out to diverse fields of study in multiple countries, Unistack is set to become the number one destination to guide students in planning, managing and executing their dream of pursuing studies abroad.

Unistack caters to all the needs of a student by providing a complete guide for planning and managing the application process. It provides users with the information regarding universities and help them shortlist universities ,make a logging sheet of the all the university applications they have applied and allow them to make changes to the status of the applications. Based on the status they can further make the list of processes to be taken care of. User can also share the experience of the application process, view the experiences of other users and comment on the posts.
