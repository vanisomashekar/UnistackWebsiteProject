Interview Tips

If you fumble and mumble for any of the questions, then chances of F1 Approval becomes extremely difficult.

If you had a solid answer to a specific question and after the consular officer denied your visa, you can’t complain that they did not ask for documents or that specific question. That’s where you should know how your interview is proceeding and tell all possible answers in the short amount of time.
